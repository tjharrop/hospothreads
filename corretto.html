Corretto Dee Why is our little neighbourhood eatery & cocktail bar.

Our goal from early on was to become a little social hub as we open 7am daily and trade through til 12am. A spot where people were as comfotable sitting on their computer sipping a coffee as they were letting their hair down with a group of friends over cocktails.

With a space designed to bring people together, the coronavirus shattered this, so we've adopted a new model, bringing togetherness to people's houses through shared style of take away drinks and food.

We still operate Wednesday to Sunday for lunch/dinner and trade for coffee & toasties daily from 7am.
